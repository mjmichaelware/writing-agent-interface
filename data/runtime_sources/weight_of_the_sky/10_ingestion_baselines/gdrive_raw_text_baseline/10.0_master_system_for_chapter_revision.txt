"something," "somehow"

9. Eliminate clichés - Find fresh way to express every cliché
10. Vary rhythm - Mix short/medium/long sentences intentionally
11. Stress sentence leads - Important info in subject position
12. Power endings - Strongest words close sentences
13. Vary openings - No 3+ consecutive sentences with same structure
