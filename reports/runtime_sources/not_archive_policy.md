They are active runtime source documents for the writing-agent interface, including typography, layer-to-cinema rendering, manuscript source state, worldbuilding/lore, synopsis/compendiums, revision snapshots, and ingestion baselines.

Canonical active root:

`data/runtime_sources/weight_of_the_sky/`

Legacy compatibility roots:

- `docs/agent_context/source_drop/gdrive_raw_manuscript_staging/gdrive_raw`
- `src/data-layer/ingestion-buffer/readme_docs`
- `src/data-layer/ingestion-buffer/gdrive_docx_intake`
